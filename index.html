WE

World Explorer for Minecraft

Read your world like a story — offline.

Utilities macOS

Point it at any Minecraft save folder or server backup and World Explorer reads the binary & JSON files locally to surface a full picture of your world — maturity score, multi-dimension map, players and play-styles, advancement progress, exploration spread, and a chronological World Story. A built-in strategy engine reads everyone's stats and suggests what to build, fight, or farm next. Nothing leaves your Mac.

CalNotes

Calendar + handwritten notes — ideas anchored to specific days.

Productivity iPhone · iPad

CalNotes pairs a clean monthly calendar with a freeform note canvas, so every idea, sketch, or to-do lives on the day it belongs to. Write naturally with Apple Pencil, organise pages into notebooks, pick a writing template — ruled, grid, or to-do — drop in a photo background, and lock anything sensitive. Export pages to PDF or PNG and sync across devices over iCloud.
